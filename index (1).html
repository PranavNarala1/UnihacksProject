Health Expansion in Rural Outskirts


Healthcare Expansion in Rural Outskirts (H.E.R.O) aims to provide policymakers and health ministries
in India insight into the most optimal locations for hospital placement in Indian cities.
Through an unsupervised machine learning model trained with population distribution models
and more, we guarantee optimial hospital placement.
